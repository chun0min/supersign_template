Stay informed

Keep updated on weather forecasts and official alerts from trusted sources. Make sure your devices are charged to receive warnings promptly. Early information helps you make safe decisions.

Create a plan

Prepare a family emergency plan with evacuation routes, meeting points, and communication methods. Ensure everyone knows their role to reduce panic during the storm.

Stock up

Gather at least 72 hours of essentials: water, food, flashlights, batteries, first-aid kit, and medications. Include items for pets or special needs. Being prepared prevents last-minute stress.

Protect
your home

Secure windows and doors, and bring outdoor items inside. Check roofs and gutters to minimize damage. These steps help reduce property loss and keep your family safe.

Stay
safe indoors

Avoid leaving home unless authorities advise evacuation. Stay away from windows, and never enter flooded areas. Staying inside greatly lowers the risk of injury.
